About Me

My name is David Madacsi, I'm originally from Mystic, Ct, a small seaside town on the East coast. I've spent many years living abroad, spending time in Hungary, Italy and the UK and have enjoyed travelling around the world. I enjoy outdoor activities and spending time with my young family. Past hobbies have included glassblowing, painting and photography.
